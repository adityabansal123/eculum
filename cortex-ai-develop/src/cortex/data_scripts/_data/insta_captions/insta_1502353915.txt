BLACKROLL® meets YOGA: the perfect synergy of movement and massage. And even more so under a blue sky.
After windsurfing, waterskiing, sailing, canoeing and SUP you might feel tight in your shoulders. It often translates to a tight trapezius muscle. It has many different function: lifting your shoulders, holding up your neck and head, and moving your shoulder blade. Thus our neck and shoulders tend to work very closely together. That's why tension in the neck usually gets into the shoulders and vice versa. Use your BLACKROLL® BALL 08 and place it between neck and shoulder (not on your shoulder blade) while going into the bridge pose. Do it carefully! Try to breathe deeply and slowly to relax. I know it hurts in the beginning ☝🏼😑, but with a conscious breathing technique you will release your fascias and the pain. Afterwards you can combine your self-massage with practicing Eagle pose and Rabbit pose.
